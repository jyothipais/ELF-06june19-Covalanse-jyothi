The cat is loving pet which
gets familiar with a person
after interaction of some minutes.
Playful is one of the
characteristics of the cat
which makes humans love them most.

Cats are independent in their life.
They do not take support or help of
others to achieve their targets.
People should be like cats to
prepare themselves enough confident
to handle things on their own.

When a cat falls from anywhere
it lands on its legs only. This
is a very important feature which
teaches us that even though we fail
we should be able to take stand
in life and never give up.
